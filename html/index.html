Good running form tips

    Head
  • Head should be in the middle of the body.
  • Head vertical movement should be as little as possible.
  • Eye should be looking forward.
    Shoulders
  • Relax shoulder.
    Hands
  • Hands should not be crossing in front of the body.
  • Arms should swing (moving forward and backward).
  • Elbow should have a gentle bend.
    Hips
  • Slightly forward
    Feet
  • Land the foot under the body.
  • >Above 165 cadences.
